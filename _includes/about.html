    Developing the Framework

    Our team combined advanced data mining techniques, natural language processing, and network analysis to create tools that map political discourse, sentiment, and influence. By leveraging technologies like BERTopic, PolitiBERT, and D3.js, we built an interactive ecosystem for political and legislative analysis.

    Empowering Political Understanding

    Gallop provides a comprehensive view of political landscapes through state-level analysis of legislative data, social media conversations, and network connections. Whether you're a voter, policymaker, or researcher, our tools deliver personalized insights into the intersection of policy and public sentiment.

    We're committed to scaling Gallop's capabilities, integrating more platforms, and enhancing prediction models to stay ahead of the evolving political climate. Our journey has just begun, and we're excited to bring transparency, clarity, and insight to politics like never before.
